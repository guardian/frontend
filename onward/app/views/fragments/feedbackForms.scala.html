How can I unsubscribe from Job alerts?

At the bottom of the email you will see an unsubscribe link. By clicking the link you will be taken to a page confirming you have unsubscribed from the emails.

How can I unsubscribe from Job Match emails?

As with Job alerts there will be an unsubscribe link at the bottom of the email. The link will take you to your Guardian account page. At the bottom of the page there are two tick boxes. Select No and Save then log back into your Jobs account. You will no longer receive any Job Match emails.
