How will it work?

Here’s an example

The refugee crisis is the biggest humanitarian challenge of our times. But even when the headlines are gone that crisis endures.

The sources of this crisis are global, but the impact is local. Guide our journalism. Focus our interest. Help us to understand how it is affecting your local area.

Tell us how refugees have been received in your area; about local organisations that have responded to the crisis in imaginative ways; introduce us to new refugee communities or local families who have opened their homes; point us towards evidence of political change at a local level.

Then read what we write as Guardian journalists, guided by your accounts, broaden and deepen our understanding of how this international crisis is experienced at a local level.

And then do something about it.
