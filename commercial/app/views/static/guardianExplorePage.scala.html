The refugee crisis is the biggest humanitarian challenge of our times. But even when the headlines are gone that crisis endures.

Guardian Explore will bring you a range of different perspectives on the crisis over the long term that will shape your view of it.

Join Patrick Kingsley as he spends months following the journey of one Syrian refugee family, sharing their – and his – experiences through weekly instalments.

Discover how refugees view their journey, understand the daily challenges they face, learn about the responses of people they encounter, and the cumulative effect of the crisis on families and individuals.

And then do something about it.
