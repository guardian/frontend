Better understand important issues through reporting, analysis and commentary expertly tailored to your level of knowledge

How will it work?

Here’s an example

The refugee crisis is the biggest humanitarian challenge of our times. But how deep is your understanding of it? And how deep would you like it to be?

Is Britain pulling its weight? Can Germany take any more? Will things get worse this summer? You can make up your own mind. You want to be sure that you haven’t missed something and you don’t want to have to wade through the acres of commentary.

You also want to know more facts and context to feel confident in voicing your opinion and know that opinion is informed. But you’re busy.

We’ll start by working out how much you know and how much you want to know. Then we’ll cut through the jargon and received wisdom to provide you with the information to take you to the next level and lay out the main arguments on all sides.
