Get your flights and tickets to the Singapore GP (and treat yourselves to some R&R afterwards)…

It's not too late to get a great deal on a package to the 2016 Formula 1 Singapore Airlines Singapore Grand Prix. A three-night race-weekend package – including flights, transfers, accommodation and race tickets – starts from £1,320 per person.* You'll receive up to 12,000 KrisFlyer miles with selected ticket categories, if you opt for the exclusive 2016 FORMULA 1 land packages, offered on the sianightrace.com website.

How to purchase your package…


1) Online

Tick off your flights, hotel airport transfers and even race tickets in one fell swoop at Singapore Airlines' sianightrace.com. Just enter your Singapore dates, pick one of the swish hotels on offer, select a GP ticket type (the default 3-Day Bay Grandstand access, or an upgraded version, subject to a top-up fee) and add optional airport transfers. Your Grand Prix travel is fixed – in just a few clicks.

Top Tip: Singapore Explorer Pass

When you're booking your flights, don't forget to add on a Singapore Explorer Pass , which will give you one-time access over 20 attractions and benefits over the duration of your purchased pass. The list includes: Alive Museum, Cable Car (two-way), Gardens By the Bay, Jurong Bird Park, the National Orchid Garden, a Singapore River Cruise, Science Centre & IMAX movie, Sentosa admission, Images of Singapore LIVE, Madame Tussauds – plus much more. Passes aren't sold in Singapore, and can only be bought from your local Singapore Airlines office, or from travel agents prior to your arrival into Singapore.
