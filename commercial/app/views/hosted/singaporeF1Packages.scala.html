How to purchase your package…

Just enter your Singapore dates, pick one of the swish hotels on offer, select a GP ticket type (the default 3-Day Bay Grandstand access, or an upgraded version, subject to a top-up fee) and add optional airport transfers. Your Grand Prix travel is fixed – in just a few clicks.

2) Travel agent

Another great option is to combine your visit with a side trip. Singapore Airlines and Trailfinders have teamed up to offer some dual-destination F1 packages – pick from Singapore and Phuket, Singapore and Borneo, or Singapore and Sydney. Wherever you decide to go, you can take advantage of big savings; there are also slashed-price flights just to Singapore, if you’ve got a one- track mind.

Why not treat yourself to a Business Class package? Prices start from £4,199 for an 11-night, twin- centre package combining Singapore and Thailand. Economy Class packages start from just £1,699 for a 10-night package combining Singapore and Bali.

Singapore Explorer Pass

When you’re booking your flights, don’t forget to add on a Singapore Explorer Pass, which will give you one-time access over 20 attractions and benefits over the duration of your purchased pass. The list includes: Alive Museum, Cable Car (two-way), Gardens By the Bay, Jurong Bird Park, the National Orchid Garden, a Singapore River Cruise, Science Centre & IMAX movie, Sentosa admission, Images of Singapore LIVE, Madame Tussauds – plus much more. Passes aren’t sold in Singapore, and can only be bought from your local Singapore Airlines office, or from travel agents prior to your arrival into Singapore.
